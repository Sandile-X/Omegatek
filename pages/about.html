Our Story

Who We Are

Founded in 2018, Omegatek Solutions started as a small repair shop in Midrand with a big vision: to provide affordable, high-quality electronics repair services with exceptional customer care. Our founder, Sandile Milanzi, began the company with a passion for technology and a commitment to excellence.

From day one, we've been driven by our core values of honesty, technical excellence, and customer satisfaction. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a team of skilled technicians and customer service professionals, all dedicated to keeping your devices working perfectly.

Our Mission

At Omegatek Solutions, our mission is simple: to provide fast, reliable, and affordable electronics repair services while treating every customer with respect and every device with care. We aim to reduce electronic waste by extending the life of your devices through quality repairs and maintenance.

Our Approach

We believe in transparency and education. When you bring your device to us, we'll explain the issue clearly, outline your repair options, and provide upfront pricing. We never recommend unnecessary repairs and pride ourselves on finding cost-effective solutions for our customers.

Our technicians undergo continuous training to stay current with the latest technologies and repair techniques, ensuring we can handle everything from vintage electronics to cutting-edge devices.
